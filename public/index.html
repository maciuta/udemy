Echo winner & 1Live Krone-nominated artist, Alle Farben, who has sold over 1.5 million records in the last three years and maintained a spot in the German Airplay Charts for 103 weeks, delivers his new hit single “Little Hollywood” right in time for this spring and summer season. With lyrics that read, “we’re living in our own little Hollywood, we’re washed up stars, everything’s gon’ be good”,” who would’ve expected any differently? After last year’s platinum & gold singles “Please Tell Rosie” & “Bad Ideas” (Airplay No. 1 in Germany) Frans Zimmer is back with another meaningful track. The composition details a lifestyle à la Hollywood with a man finding himself in his own cosmos while getting notably cast in stone. The philosophy of the song connects straight to the mindset of the Berlin based DJ and producer, who also, as usual, about to play over 100 shows around the world in 2017.
